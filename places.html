Roman Theatre


Category: Theatre

Rating: 4.3 ★★★★☆


The Roman Theatre in downtown Amman, Jordan. Built in the 2nd century AD when the city was known as Philadelphia. This ancient theatre reflects the grandeur of Roman urban life. Visitors can enjoy views over the city, and visit small museums. Several cultural events are hosted there as well.

Parliament Building


Category: Historical Monument

Rating: 4.6 ★★★★☆


The Hungarian Parliament Building is located along the Danube River in central Budapest. Built in the late 19th century, it is one of Hungary's most important national symbols. Visitors can admire its grand architecture, take guided tours inside, and enjoy beautiful river views.

Category: Historical Monument

Rating: 4.6 ★★★★☆


Fisherman's Bastion is located on Castle Hill in Budapest. Built in the late 19th century, it was designed as a viewing terrace and symbolizes the city's medieval past. Visitors come for its towers, white stone design, and panoramic views of the Danube and Parliament.
