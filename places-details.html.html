Parliament Building---Budapest

Catagory:Architectural and Historical Ratings:4.8 (25,911)
The Hungarian Parliament Building is one of the most iconic landmarks in Budapest and on of the largest in the whole world. The construction of the building began in 1885 and was completed in 1904, designed by architect Imre Steindl in a Neo-Gothic style inspired by the British Parliament.
opening hours: 8:00Am - 6:00Pm
entry fee: 14300 HUF (30 JOD)
